Privacy Notice

How FitLife Time Tracker handles your data under GDPR.

Data we collect
  • Employee ID, full name, department, contractual monthly target hours.
  • Daily working hours: start, end, break, calculated net hours.
  • HR action audit log (views, acknowledgements, reminders, exports).
Purpose

To fulfil the employer's legal obligation to document working time under German labour law and to provide employees with transparency over their own working time records.

Access
  • Employees can see and edit only their own time records.
  • HR staff can view aggregated and per-employee time records for payroll review.
Retention

Time records are retained for the statutory period and then deleted. Employees may request erasure of records no longer legally required.

Security
  • Session timeout: 30 minutes of inactivity.
  • Role-based access control enforced server-side.
  • All HR access is audit-logged.
